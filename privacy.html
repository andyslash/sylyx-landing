The short version: Sylyx is a local-first desktop app. We collect only your email address when you join the waitlist. We never sell it, never use it to train AI, and never share it with advertisers. That's it.

01

Who we are

Sylyx is an open-source writing studio for novelists, built and maintained by Andy Randrianirina (contact@sylyx.app). The project is hosted publicly on GitHub at github.com/andyslash/sylyx-desktop.

For the purposes of data protection law (including the EU General Data Protection Regulation — GDPR), the data controller is the individual named above.

02

What we collect

When you join our waitlist on this page, we collect:

  • Your email address. This is the only personal data we collect.

We do not collect your name, location, device information, or any usage analytics through the waitlist form. The Sylyx desktop application itself does not send any data back to us — it is fully local-first. Your files, manuscripts, and writing never leave your machine.

03

How we use it

We use your email address for one purpose only:

  • To notify you when Sylyx is ready to launch. We will send you a single email (or a very small number of emails) when the app is available for download.

We will not send you marketing emails, newsletters, or promotional content beyond what you signed up for. You can unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email we send you.

The legal basis for processing your email address is your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR), given when you submit the waitlist form.

04

Storage & security

Waitlist email addresses are stored securely with our email service provider. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

We retain your email address until the waitlist purpose has been fulfilled (i.e., the launch email has been sent), after which you may request deletion at any time. We will not retain data longer than necessary.

05

Third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may use a third-party email delivery service (such as Resend, Mailchimp, or ConvertKit) solely to send you the waitlist notification. These services process your email address on our behalf under a data processing agreement and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose.

We never use your email address — or any data derived from your use of Sylyx — to train artificial intelligence models.

The Sylyx GitHub repository is public. If you star or contribute to the repository, your GitHub username is visible per GitHub's own privacy policy, which is separate from this policy.

06

Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with similar data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — You may ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — You may ask us to delete your data at any time.
  • Right to restrict processing — You may ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — You may request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to withdraw consent — You may withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (e.g., the CNIL in France).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Cookies

This landing page does not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar third-party tracking tools.

No cookies are set when you visit this page or submit the waitlist form.

08

Continued use of the waitlist or the Sylyx application after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
